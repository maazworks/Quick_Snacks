Grilled Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:
300 g chicken cubes 2 tbsp yogurt 1 tsp garlic paste 1 tsp ginger paste 1 tsp paprika / chili powder Salt & pepper 1 tbsp oil Bell peppers & onion (optional)

Time:
30 minutes (incl. marination)

Recipe:
Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, oil (15 min). Thread onto skewers with veggies. Grill or pan-grill till cooked and slightly charred.

Calories:
280–320 kcal per serving (3–4 skewers)
