Green Detox Smoothie

Ingredients:
1 cup spinach or kale (washed) ½ green apple, chopped ½ banana (for creaminess) ½ cup cucumber, chopped 1 cup water or coconut water Juice of ½ lemon 1 tsp grated ginger (optional)

Time:
5 minutes

Recipe:
Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust sweetness or thickness. Serve immediately.

Calories:
120-200kcal per serving
