Chickpea Chaat

Ingredients:
Boiled chickpeas (kabuli chana) 1 cup Onion (finely chopped) ¼ cup Tomato (finely chopped) ¼ cup Cucumber (finely chopped) ¼ cup Green chili (optional) 1 small Lemon juice 1–1½ tbsp Chaat masala 1 tsp Roasted cumin powder ½ tsp Red chili powder ¼ tsp Salt To taste Fresh coriander 1–2 tbsp

Time:
Total Time 10–25 minutes

Recipe:
Prepare chickpeas If using dried chickpeas, soak overnight and pressure cook until soft. Drain well. Mix vegetables In a bowl, add chickpeas, onion, tomato, cucumber, green chili, and coriander. Season Add lemon juice, chaat masala, cumin powder, chili powder, salt, and olive oil. Toss & serve Mix gently and serve immediately.

Calories:
Calories 180–220 kcal Protein 8–10 g Carbohydrates 30 g Fiber 8 g Fat 3–4 g
