Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:
Ripe banana, wheat flour or oats flour, milk, baking powder, butter or oil

Time:
20 minutes

Recipe:
Mash the banana well, then mix with flour, baking powder, and milk to form a smooth batter. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it. Pour small portions of batter and cook on low–medium heat until bubbles form. Flip and cook the other side until golden. Serve warm.

Calories:
180–220 kcal (2–3 pancakes)
