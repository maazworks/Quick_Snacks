Vegetable Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:
2 bread slices ½ cup grated cheese 2–3 tbsp finely chopped vegetables (cucumber, tomato, capsicum, onion – optional) Butter Salt & black pepper (optional)

Time:
15 minutes

Recipe:
Mix chopped vegetables with grated cheese and a pinch of salt and pepper. Spread butter on bread slices and add the vegetable–cheese filling. Cover with another slice and toast or grill until golden and cheese melts. Cut and serve warm.

Calories:
~230–260 kcal
